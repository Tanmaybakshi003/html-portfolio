ONE OF MY fAVOURITE HOLLYWOOD ACTOR

CHRISTIAN BALE


Christian Charles Philip Bale is an English actor. Known for his versatility and physical transformations for his roles, he has been a leading man in films of several genres. He has received various accolades, including an Academy Award and two Golden Globe Awards.

SOME OF HIS FAMOUS MOVIES ARE -

  • American psycho

    • IMDB REVIEW

    Bane, an imposing terrorist, attacks Gotham City and disrupts its eight-year-long period of peace. This forces Bruce Wayne to come out of hiding and don the cape and cowl of Batman again.

  • The machinist
